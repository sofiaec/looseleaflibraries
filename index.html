Diverse Book Recommendations

Why do we need to read diverse books in the first place?

People in minority groups have been underrepresented for a long time. They never get to see people with certain characteristics reflected in a positive and unharmful way in books. This is partly because of the publishing industry only publishing books from white authors but we can still do everything we can as readers to get these voices heard. We have a certain responsibility as conssumers of these products to make sure that they are inclusive. If we promote books we diverse voices in them my hope is one day they will become our mainstream books. So take a look at these reccomendations and happy reading!

Where did these recs come from?

I created a tag on instagram where people shared their favorite diverse recommendations. I got so many good recs that I felt like I need to make a place to put them all together for easy access for everyone. Each book here was recommended as a favorite book from a bookstagrammer! Having everything in one place definitely always makes me feel a lot more organized and willing to take in the info so I hope this helps get you motivated to read some diverse books!
